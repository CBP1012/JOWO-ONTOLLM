Committee Members

John Beverley
University at Buffalo

John Beverley is an Assistant Professor of Philosophy at SUNY Buffalo and Co-Director of the National Center for Ontological Research, leveraging formal logic and knowledge representation to develop ontologies that enhance AI applications in domains from infectious disease to healthy aging.

Peter Elkin
University at Buffalo; Chair, Biomedical Informatics

Peter Elkin, MD, MACP, FACMI, FNYAM, is Distinguished Professor and Chair of the Department of Biomedical Informatics at UB’s Jacobs School of Medicine, renowned for pioneering work in clinical ontology, natural language processing, and health data standards with over 200 peer-reviewed publications.

Jeremy Ravenel
Naas.ai, Founder & CEO

Jeremy Ravenel is the Founder & CEO of Naas.ai, a universal open-source data platform empowering users worldwide to democratize data and AI through low-code templates and generative AI integrations.

Ken Archer
Microsoft

Ken Archer is Principal in Responsible AI at Microsoft and a Ph.D. researcher in Philosophy, Cognitive Science & AI at Linköping University, focusing on phenomenology of probability and ethical AI design.

Alcides Lopes
Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul

Alcides Lopes a PhD graduate of UFRGS, researching ontology-based information retrieval in the oil and gas domain with interests in semantic-driven AI, knowledge-based systems, and machine learning.
